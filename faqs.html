Arrival & Departure

What are the check-in and check-out times?
Check-in is from 15:00. Check-out is by 11:00. Early check-in and late check-out may be available on request, subject to availability. Please contact us in advance if you need special arrangements.
Can I store my luggage before check-in or after check-out?
Yes, luggage storage is complimentary for all guests, whether you're arriving early or extending your day after check-out. Our front desk team will take care of your bags.
Is there parking available at the hotel?
Stories Hotel does not have private parking. However, several public car parks are available within walking distance of Király utca. We are happy to recommend the nearest options on request. Budapest's public transport is excellent — the hotel is very well connected.
How do I get from Budapest Airport to the hotel?
The hotel is approximately 30–40 minutes from Budapest Liszt Ferenc International Airport. We recommend the 100E airport bus (direct to Deák Ferenc tér, then a short walk or taxi), a taxi, or a pre-booked transfer. We can arrange airport transfers — please contact us.

Rooms

How many room types do you offer?
We offer six distinct room categories: Presidential Suite (165–180 m²), King Suite with Jacuzzi (85 m²), Junior Suite (65–75 m²), Deluxe Room (34–45 m²), Deluxe Queen Room (30 m²), and Superior Room (17 m²). Each is individually designed. View all rooms →
Do rooms have air conditioning?
Yes, all rooms are fully air-conditioned with individual climate control, allowing you to set your preferred temperature throughout your stay.
Is WiFi available in the rooms?
Complimentary high-speed WiFi is available throughout the hotel, including all guest rooms, common areas, and via Create 26 coworking access.
Are pets allowed?
We welcome small, well-behaved pets in select rooms. Please notify us at the time of booking. A small daily pet fee applies. Please contact us before your visit to confirm arrangements.

Amenities

Do guests have access to a gym?
Yes — through our exclusive partnership with GoActive, Stories Hotel guests enjoy complimentary access to a world-class fitness facility in central Budapest. State-of-the-art equipment, personal training, and group classes are all available. Learn more →
Is there coworking access for guests?
Stories guests have exclusive access to Create 26, a boutique coworking space sharing our building. Private desks, meeting rooms, high-speed WiFi, and specialty coffee are all included. Learn more →
Is there a bar at the hotel?
Our Bar & Social Lounge is located within the Twenty Six Budapest building and is open to guests and the public. Curated cocktails, natural wines, and a thoughtful bar food menu are available daily.

Dining

Is breakfast included?
Breakfast is available as an add-on to your booking. It is served in the Twenty Six restaurant from 07:00 to 11:00. Our breakfast features local Hungarian produce, daily-baked pastries, and a seasonal menu. Please indicate at booking whether you'd like breakfast included.
Can dietary requirements be accommodated?
Absolutely. Please inform us of any dietary requirements, allergies, or preferences at the time of booking or no later than 48 hours before your arrival. We'll ensure the kitchen is prepared to accommodate you.
Is room service available?
Selected in-room dining options are available. Please speak with our front desk team upon arrival for current room service hours and menu options.

Location

Where exactly is the hotel located?
Stories Hotel is located at Király utca 26, 1061 Budapest, in the heart of the VI. District — one of Budapest's most vibrant neighbourhoods, close to Andrássy Avenue, the Opera House, and the Jewish Quarter. Explore the neighbourhood →
How close is the hotel to the city's main attractions?
The hotel is within easy walking or public transport reach of Budapest's most celebrated sights — St. Stephen's Basilica (10 min walk), Heroes' Square (20 min walk), the Danube riverbanks (15 min), and the Great Market Hall (20 min).
What public transport is nearby?
The hotel is excellently connected. Nyugati metro station (line M3) is a 5-minute walk. Multiple tram and bus lines stop nearby on Teréz körút and Bajcsy-Zsilinszky út. View transport details →
