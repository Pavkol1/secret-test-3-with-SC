Stories Hotel/Neighbourhood

The City at
Your Doorstep

Stories Hotel sits in the VI. District — Budapest's most storied, walkable, and culturally rich neighbourhood. Everything the city has to offer is minutes away.

Our Location

Király utca 26,
the VI. District

Budapest's inner VI. District — known as Terézváros — is a neighbourhood of grand boulevard architecture, tree-lined streets, and an endless stream of cultural life. From our front door, you're steps from Andrássy Avenue, the Opera House, and the Jewish Quarter.

We share our building with Twenty Six Budapest — the celebrated restaurant that has become one of the city's favourite gathering places. And Create 26 coworking is right upstairs. This is not just a location. It is a destination.

Around the Hotel

What's Nearby

01

Hungarian State Opera

8 min walk

One of Europe's most beautiful neo-Renaissance opera houses. Guided tours, world-class performances, and an architectural treasure on Andrássy Avenue.

02

St. Stephen's Basilica

10 min walk

Budapest's grandest church, with panoramic views from the dome terrace. The neoclassical interior and the Holy Right relic draw visitors from around the world.

03

House of Terror Museum

12 min walk

A powerful memorial to Hungary's 20th-century history, housed in the former Arrow Cross and communist secret police headquarters on Andrássy Avenue.

04

Hungarian Jewish Museum

15 min walk

Adjacent to the Great Synagogue — Europe's largest — this museum documents the rich and complex history of Hungarian Jewish life and culture.

05

Heroes' Square

25 min walk / 10 min metro

The symbolic heart of Hungary, flanked by the Museum of Fine Arts and the Palace of Art. The Millennium Monument is one of Budapest's most iconic landmarks.

06

New York Palace & Café

20 min walk

One of the most opulent cafés in the world — a gilded, frescoed masterpiece that has been the meeting place of Budapest's literary and artistic elite since 1894.

01

Twenty Six Budapest

In our building

Our home restaurant — a vibrant dining destination serving creative, market-driven cuisine in a lush, design-forward setting. Breakfast, lunch, and dinner daily.

02

Café Kor

5 min walk

A beloved neighbourhood bistro serving simple, seasonal Hungarian and European food in an unpretentious setting — a favourite of locals and savvy visitors alike.

03

Trofea Grill

12 min walk

A celebrated all-you-can-eat restaurant with an extensive selection of Hungarian and international dishes. Perfect for a convivial group dinner.

04

Gerbeaud

12 min walk

Budapest's most famous café and confectionery on Vörösmarty Square. Exquisite pastries, hot chocolate, and a history dating back to 1858.

01

City Park (Városliget)

25 min walk / 10 min metro

Budapest's beloved urban park, home to the Széchenyi Thermal Bath, Heroes' Square, Vajdahunyad Castle, and the Budapest Zoo. A green escape in the heart of the city.

02

Andrássy Avenue

8 min walk

Budapest's UNESCO-listed grand boulevard — lined with plane trees, mansions, embassies, and boutiques. One of Central Europe's great urban promenades.

03

Margaret Island

20 min by tram

A car-free island in the Danube offering gardens, a running track, thermal baths, and peaceful walks — one of Budapest's most cherished green spaces.

Getting Around

Transport

Metro

Nyugati metro station (M3 blue line) is a 5-minute walk. Deák Ferenc tér interchange (M1, M2, M3) is 10 minutes on foot or one stop by metro.

Tram & Bus

Tram 4/6 (the city's busiest line) runs along Teréz körút, one block away. Multiple bus lines connect the hotel to all parts of Budapest.

Airport Transfer

Budapest Airport is 30–40 minutes away. The 100E bus runs direct to Deák tér. We can arrange private airport transfers — please enquire when booking.
